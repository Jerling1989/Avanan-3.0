Make EDUs a No Phishing Zone with Avanan

Thursday, September 26th at 2:00pm ET

Educational Institutions are under attack. Phishing attacks target students, staff, and alumni, who are caught off-guard and tricked into giving up login credentials. According to the 2019 Verizon Data Breach Investigation Report, “Web Application Attacks accounted for roughly one-quarter of breaches in the Education vertical” which are mostly due to the "frequent compromise of cloud-based mail services via phishing links to phony login pages."

Avanan has come to the service of these institutions by offering creative licensing models that make protecting their diverse user base in the most cost-efficient manner possible. Current clients range from small colleges to major university systems, who can get up and running quickly with a one-click cloud deployment.

Join us for this webinar, where the relationship management team and our engineering lead who work with so many of our educational institution clients will walk through the key aspects of our solution.
